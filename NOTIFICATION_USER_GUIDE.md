## Troubleshooting

### I don't see a notification for my friend's post
**Solution**: 
- Make sure you're connected with your friend
- Check if they toggled notifications off

### The bell shows an unread count but I've read them all
**Solution**:
- Click "Mark all read" button in the dropdown
- Refresh the page to reset the count

### Notifications aren't updating
**Solution**:
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Log out and back in

### I want to turn off notifications for everything
**Solution**:
- Click the bell on your profile
- Toggle to "Notifications Off"
- This only affects post notifications

## FAQ

**Q: Will I get a notification if someone likes their own post?**
A: No, you only get notifications for actions from other users.

**Q: Can I get notifications on my phone?**
A: Yes, if you have the web app open in your browser, notifications will appear.

**Q: How many notifications can I store?**
A: The system shows your 20 most recent notifications. Older ones are archived.

**Q: What if I don't want notifications from a specific person?**
A: Currently, notifications are based on connections. You can remove the connection.

**Q: Do notifications work if I'm offline?**
A: Notifications are queued and will appear when you go back online.
